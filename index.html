About Me

Oceanic & Atmospheric researcher specializing in subseasonal-to-seasonal (S2S) precipitation predictability, with a focus on the Madden-Julian Oscillation (MJO) over the Maritime Continent (MC). My research integrates land-atmosphere interactions, soil moisture variability, topographical effects, and high-resolution modeling to improve our understanding of tropical convection. Experienced in numerical weather and climate models (MOM, WRF), high-performance computing (HPC), and analyzing observations, satellite and reanalysis datasets. Passionate about advancing climate model development through dynamical core analysis and machine learning (ML) applications.
